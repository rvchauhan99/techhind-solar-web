| Action Type | Button Variant Name | Usage Examples | Background (Hex) | Text (Hex) | Border (Hex) | Hover (Hex) | Disabled | Icon Rule |
|-------------|---------------------|----------------|------------------|------------|--------------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Primary | `default` | Save, Submit, Create, Update, Next | #2563eb | #ffffff | — | #1d4ed8 | 50% opacity | Allowed; left of label |
| Secondary | `outline` | Cancel, Back, Close, Export, Reset | #ffffff | #1e293b | #cbd5e1 | #f1f5f9 | 50% opacity | Allowed; left of label |
| Destructive | `destructive` | Delete, Remove | #dc2626 | #ffffff | — | #b91c1c | 50% opacity | Allowed; left of label |
